## ClouDNS Introduction

ClouDNS started its journey back in March 2010, founded by Boyan Peychev with just four free DNS servers spread across the UK, USA, and Bulgaria. What began as a modest project has grown into one of Europe's largest providers of managed DNS services. Today, the company handles over 8 billion DNS queries daily and serves customers in more than 11,200 cities across 210 countries.

The company operates an impressive global Anycast DNS network spanning 65+ data centers across six continents. This infrastructure ensures lightning-fast DNS resolution with minimal latency, no matter where your users are located. ClouDNS specializes in several DNS services including Premium DNS, GeoDNS, DDoS Protected DNS, and Anycast DNS, all backed by industry-leading uptime guarantees.

What makes ClouDNS stand out is its flexibility. Whether you're running a personal blog or managing enterprise-level infrastructure, they've got options. Their free tier offers genuine value with 4 DNS servers, while their paid plans scale from small businesses to enterprise needs. The platform supports every DNS record type you'd need - A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, TXT, SRV, and more specialized records like ALIAS and CAA.

The company is ISO 27001 certified and ICANN accredited, meeting high standards for security and compliance. They've built their own synchronization system that updates your DNS zones simultaneously across all Points of Presence (POPs), so once you configure your zones, everything just works. Their DDoS protection infrastructure is self-developed and designed to handle various attack types, which is critical since DNS is often a primary target for attackers.

ClouDNS also offers 24/7 live chat support, which users consistently praise in reviews. The support team responds quickly and actually knows their stuff - they're not just reading from scripts. This level of service applies even to free plan users during their trial period, which is somewhat unusual in the hosting industry.
